About

We can help you unlock the secrets of your photographs without putting your data or images at risk.

ImageMetrics gets the data behind your images. This is done in a way that doesn't expose your images to others and your images' data isn't stored on a server for others to find. You have the option to export your data for you to store locally, but it is not stored by ImageMetrics.

ImageMetrics will let you know if your image was taken with a cellphone and tell you which model, GoPro, or a digital camera, such as a Nikon or Canon. It will let you know the software version of the device that captured the picture.

ImageMetrics will pin-point the map coordinates of where you image was captured.

ImageMetrics gives you the date and time the image was captured. It also provides the date and time when the image was digitized.

ImageMetrics provides various information about the camera's aperture for your specific image. It will provide the details about the aperature's value and the aperture's speed.

ImageMetrics provides information on shutter speed, exposure mode, image file name, ISO speeds, compression, image dimensions, sharpness, saturation, and many more data points about your image.
